Hamburger

According to Wikipedia, the Hamburger first appeared between the year 1885 and 1904 with many claiming responsibility for creating possibly the most iconic food in history. The Hamburger is most comonly made with the combination of ground beef, cheese, assorted vegetables, cheese, and assoreted condiments.

Ingridients

  • Ground Beef
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatos
  • Onions
  • Mayonaise

Directions

  1. Shape Ground Beef into the shape of a Hamburger patty
  2. Cook patty in a pan on medium heat till patty is brown and crispy on both sides
  3. Seperate the top and bottom hamburger buns
  4. Place one leaf of lettuce on the bottom bun
  5. placed cooked patty on top of lettuce
  6. Place one slice of cheese on top of cooked patty
  7. Place 2 slices of tomato overlapping each other to cover the most possible surface area of cooked patty
  8. Place desired amount of onions on top of Tomatos
  9. Grab a butter knife and spread mayonaise on top bun
  10. Place top bun on top of onions
  11. Take a bite and enjoy!!!!!!
