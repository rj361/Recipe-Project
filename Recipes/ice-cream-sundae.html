Ingridients

  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Choclate Ice Cream
  • Choclate Syrup
  • Cherry
  • Choclate Sprinkles
  • Crushed Nuts
  • Whipped Cream

Directions

  1. Place one scoop of vanilla ice cream in a sundae bowl
  2. PLace one scoop of choclate ice cream in the sundae bowl
  3. Pour Choclate syrup in sundae covering the ice cream
  4. Sprinkle the crushed nuts over the bowl
  5. Sprinkle choclate sprinkles over the bowl
  6. Cover Ice Cream in whipped cream
  7. Place cherry in cneter of bowl
  8. Take a bite and enjoy!!!!!!
